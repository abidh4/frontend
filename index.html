About Comilla University

Comilla University (CoU) is a prominent public university in Bangladesh, dedicated to academic excellence, research, and innovation. Established to foster knowledge and leadership, CoU offers a vibrant campus life, diverse academic programs, and a commitment to nurturing future leaders. The university is renowned for its modern facilities, experienced faculty, and a supportive learning environment.

Facilities at CoU

Modern Library

A well-equipped library with a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources to support students and researchers.

Student Halls

Comfortable and secure residential halls for students, fostering a vibrant and inclusive campus community.

Laboratories

State-of-the-art laboratories for science, engineering, and technology students to enhance practical learning.

Sports Complex

Extensive sports facilities including fields, courts, and a gymnasium to promote physical fitness and teamwork.
